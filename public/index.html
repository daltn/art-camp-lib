About

Also mixed in with this collection of references is much of the work that came out of our the long creative process (photographs, typography, layouts, language, renderings, drawings, stamps…).

Our hope is that this document can be of use to those people in the future whom will be assigned the task of producing new creative work for Dig.

Our goal in presenting two images next to each other, or ‘combinations,’ is to help stimulate our minds and spark new ideas. By placing two images together that are distinct yet complementary, we begin to create links and bridges that can result in other new and unpredictable ideas.
